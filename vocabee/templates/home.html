VocaBee

Vocabee is meant to be the next-generation solution for your Japanese language needs. Instead of traditional textbooks, VocaBee makes full use of the power of modern technology to greatly enhance your study efficiency, by adding features that real life methods can’t accomplish.

Why this website

In the Japanese language there’s commonly agreed to only be one solid exam to test your knowledge, the Japanese Language Proficiency Test, or commonly abbreviated to JLPT. The JLPT is a worldwide written exam testing your knowledge of vocabulary, grammar, kanji and listening skills. Over the many years of language learners, a great number of tools to help you master this difficult language have appeared, some of them incredibly well thought out. As a learner myself however, I always felt slightly wronged by the current methods of learning vocabulary. I always felt like I was limited by tools that were either outdated, too pricey or just plain outdated. This is why I felt inspired to build VocaBee, a website to learn Japanese in a modern way, or at the least provide you with an easier to browse vocabulary list.

Future features

Although rather bare at the moment, lots of promising features are planned. Notable ones are custom anki decks, a highly customizable vocabulary quiz and an effortless way to create your own vocabulary lists.
