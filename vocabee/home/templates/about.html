About

As the creator of this website, I've had my fair share of language learning experiences. In my classes I've never really struggled with learning characters, or speaking but vocabulary consistently failed to stick. I've always attributed this to the fact that I was learning from a printed vocabulary book, no examples, no assisting techniques, just plain memorizing. I knew I could do something about this, using all the modern techniques in the book to make our journey a little easier.
