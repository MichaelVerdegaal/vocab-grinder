About

I've had my fair share of different language learning methods and experiences. In my own classes I've never really struggled with learning characters, or speaking, but vocabulary consistently failed to stick. I've always attributed this to the fact that I was learning from a printed vocabulary book, no examples, no assisting techniques, no nothing, just plain old memorizing. I knew I could do something about this, considering all the wonderful techniques we have available now. So I put my website creation skills to the test, and made VocaBee, just to make your journey a little easier.

~Michael (Creator of VocaBee)
